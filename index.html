About Insulin Doses Calculator

Insulin Doses Calculator is a simple, intuitive app designed to support people living with diabetes in making informed decisions around insulin dosing. Built for both Type 1 and insulin-dependent Type 2 diabetes, the app helps users better understand how food intake, blood glucose levels, and personal insulin settings work together.

The app allows users to log meals, view nutritional information, and receive insulin dose recommendations based on their individual insulin-to-carbohydrate ratios and correction factors. By combining structured data with intelligent calculations, Insulin Doses Calculator aims to reduce guesswork and bring greater confidence to everyday diabetes management.

With a clean, user-friendly interface, the app makes it easy to:

  • Record meals and nutritional values
  • Track blood glucose history
  • View meal history and insights
  • View trends over time with charts and graphs, 7, 14, 30 & 90 day views
  • Generate dose guidance tailored to the user
  • Generate enabled Clinician Summary or item by item report with the Clinician.

Insulin Doses Calculator is designed as a supportive tool, not a replacement for medical advice. All recommendations are intended to complement guidance provided by healthcare professionals, empowering users to stay informed, organised, and in control of their routine.

The app is currently in development and will be available for download in the future.
